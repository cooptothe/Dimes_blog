About D1ME

D1ME is a DJ and visual artist from New Orleans, LA.

Her work is a reflection of her personal liberation through the arts and explores matters of innocence, identity, and the interplay of sound and emotion.

While her music selection is an avant garde oration of her roots as a Black artist from The South, D1ME’s thoughtful curation celebrates Black and POC artistry in electronic music globally.

As a leading force in New Orleans’ music scene, D1ME is unmatched for her selection in the city. D1ME co-founded the Techno trio N*S*F*W with DJs Lady Lavender and Gabriela, with the goal of reshaping how art is curated and enjoyed on the dancefloor.

D1ME’s solo performances range from House to Techno, Ghetto Tech, Footwork, Broken Beat and Club music. A gem in her community, D1ME is dedicated to sharing her unique artistic vision with the world.
