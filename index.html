About Me

D1ME, is a versatile DJ and visual artist born and raised in New Orleans, LA. Rooted in personal liberation and inspired by her love of travel, her intentional music selections span diverse influences from her upbringing within a cultural mecca. Rapidly gaining prominence in New Orleans' music scene, she later co-founded the techno trio N*S*F*W alongside Djs, Lady Lavender and Gabriela, with the intent to redefine women's roles in music, fusing photography, design, and music together. Her solo sets amplify the sound of Black and other POC artists that can be underrepresented within electronic music across diasporas; each track selected carries a sense of intention and depth. D1ME's passion and work ethic make her a thriving force in her community, eager to share her unique perspective globally.
